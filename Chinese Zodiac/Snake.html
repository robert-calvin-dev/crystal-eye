Snake

Personality Traits

People born in the Year of the Snake are intelligent, wise, and charming. They are deep thinkers who speak very little but are always planning and plotting. Snakes are materialistic creatures, preferring to surround themselves with the finest that life has to offer. This sign is keenly aware of their surroundings and highly intuitive.

Strengths

Wisdom: Snakes are known for their wisdom and philosophical approach to life.

Charm: They are highly charismatic and naturally able to attract and engage others.

Determination: Once they have decided on a course of action, Snakes are incredibly focused and will pursue their goals without distraction.

Business acumen: They have a sharp business sense and are good at making deals.

Discretion: Snakes are discreet with their words and often keep their thoughts to themselves.

Weaknesses

Jealousy: They can be jealous and suspicious of others, which can lead to strained relationships.

Manipulativeness: Snakes can sometimes manipulate situations and people to their advantage.

Indecisiveness: Despite their outer confidence, they can be unsure in decision-making, often wavering when they face critical choices.

Career

Snakes do well in careers that involve serious contemplation and strategic planning. Ideal careers might include scientist, analyst, investigator, painter, potter, jeweler, astrologer, magician, and sociologist.

Health

Snakes need to manage their stress levels to avoid health issues. Activities like yoga, meditation, or long walks are beneficial for maintaining their mental and physical health.

Cultural Significance

In Chinese culture, the Snake is seen as a symbol of intelligence, mystery, and wisdom. It is associated with various mystical and spiritual qualities.
